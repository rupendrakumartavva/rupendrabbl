Add An Existing Business License to My DC Business Center Account

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page displays for verification the BBL information that corresponds to the information you entered before adding the license to your My BBLs Page.

The details of the Basic Business License that corresponds to the information entered is displayed for review.

Take time to ensure that all components of the license information is correct and appropriately corresponds to the BBL you wish to renew.

If the BBL information is incorrect, select the button and contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.

If the BBL information is correct, select the button to add a record of this BBL to your My BBL's page.

  • You will be navigated to your My BBLs page where a record of the BBL has been successfully added.
  • If you have multiple licenses eligible for renewal, the license soonest to expire will be at the top of the page.
  • To initiate the renewal process for the BBL, select the link adjacent to the License you wish to renew.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
