Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is to verify that the business and/or business owner meets the District of Columbia financial standards for obtaining a Basic Business License.

  • Type your full name in the first field .
  • Select the owner status that applies to you from the drop down menu .
  • Enter the FEIN or SSN number that corresponds to your business, as indicated on your Pre-Application questions.
  • Check the box to indicate you understand your legal responsibility for the information provided.
  • Attest to the accuracy and veracity of the financial information provided by typing your legally binding signature in the field at the bottom of the page. . The content of the name you type in the field at the bottom of the page must match the content of your name you typed at the top of the page.
  • Complete the Clean Hands Attestation on the day you pay for and submit your Application. If you save and return to your Draft Application on another day, you will need to check and sign the Attestation form again.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
