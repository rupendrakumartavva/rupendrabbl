This page is used to capture the Basic Business License number of the company that employs the individual and the employee's personal information.

Individual licenses are to be applied for by the company that employs the individual.

Each employee will require a separate Individual Business License and therefore a separate Individual license application will be needed for each employee.

Fill in the blanks to continue with the application.

Upon approval, the individual employee must visit DCRA to obtain an Identification Card.
