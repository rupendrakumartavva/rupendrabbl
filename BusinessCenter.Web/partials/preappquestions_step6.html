License Category and Fee Confirmation

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page summarizes your answers to the Pre-Application Questions and displays the fees associated with your BBL application.


Based on your responses to the Pre-Application Questions, your Basic Business License will include the Business License Categories and Fees listed on this page.

Your answers to the Pre-Application Questions enable the system to generate a "Checklist" of the information and documents you will need to complete your application for a Basic Business License.

Carefully review the information presented.

  • To Display Details of Licenses and Fees use the link.
  • To make a change, select the button.
  • To proceed to the next step of the application and generate a "Checklist" of the information and documents you will need to complete your application for a Basic Business License select the button.

    *Note* Once you leave this page and select the button to generate your "Checklist," you will no longer be able to make any changes to the Pre-Application information provided.

    If you notice an error in an answer to a Pre-Application Question AFTER you have generated your Checklist:

    • 1.   Return to the My Basic Business License Page
    • 2.   Select the button on the erroneous record
    • 3.   Start a new application with the button.
  • To exit the application and NOT save, select the button.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
