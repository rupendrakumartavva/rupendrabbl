Pre-Application Questions

Help With This Page (Click Here)

The Pre-Application Questions collect the user's information to determine the requirements for their Business License before they start the application.


*NOTE* Pre-Application questions about license categories, business location and structure will vary based upon the type of Basic Business License being applied for and the answers the user provides. You will only be asked about the license categories and questions that pertain to your license application.

Glossary of Pre-Application Terms and Features

  • Primary Business Activity: Select one option. Click on the (Look Up Business Activities and License Categories Here) to see the Primary License Categories associated with the Primary Business Activities.
  • Primary Business License Category: Select one option.
  • Secondary Business License Categories: If applicable, select all options that apply.
    • If no secondary categories apply, select the button to proceed to the next step.
    • If you select one or more secondary categories, select the button to proceed to the next step.
  • General Business Sub Categories: If applicable, select one option.
  • Corporate Registration: State if the business you are applying for already has registered with the DCRA's Corporations Division
  • Business Structure: Select one option from the pull down menu.
  • Trade Name: If applicable, enter your business' Trade Name (also referred to as a "Doing Business As (DBA)" Name or Alias in other jurisdictions).
  • Quantity: If applicable, enter the quantity associated with your business (e.g. Hotel Rooms, Restaurant Seats, Gasoline Pumps)
  • Duration of License: Select if you want your license to last two or four years before expiring. Please note the expiration date on the supporting documents needed to qualify for a basic business license.
  • Location: Select whether the business that you are applying for is Located in the District of Columbia.
  • Home Based Business: If applicable, designate if your business will be located in your home or not.
  • Home Occupancy Permit (HOP): If your business is Home Based, designate if you already have an HOP or not.
  • Tax Identification Number: Select whether the tax ID associated with your business is an FEIN or an SSN.

To revise information on a previous page select the button.

To continue to the next page select the button.

To exit the application but NOT save any information, select the button.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.

Please select your business activity, license categories, and provide answers to all Pre-Application Questions. Based upon your responses, the system will generate a checklist of the information and documents you will need to complete and submit your application.
