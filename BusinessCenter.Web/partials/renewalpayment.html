Basic Business License Renewal Payment

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is to make payment to renew a Basic Business License.

The grand total cost to renew your license is displayed at the top of the page.

*Note* Any late fees associate with the renew are also displayed.

Select the type of credit card you will use from the pull down menu of accepted cards.

Enter your credit card number, expiration date and security code in the appropriate fields.

Enter and re-enter the email address to receive your payment receipt.

Use the radio buttons to select the billing address for your credit card, which can be the displayed address from your license application or another address that you will be asked to type into the appropriate fields.

In order to move forward from this page you must select the box stating that you making any false statements in your renewal may make you subject to criminal prosecution.

Select the button to complete the transaction.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
