Business Information

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is used to validate the Home Occupancy Permit (HOP) for the business to be licensed

  • Enter your Home Occupancy Permit Number in the box as it appears on your the document, to include letters and numbers.
  • Select the button.
  • A screen will unfold that displays the Business Premise address that corresponds to that Home Occupancy Permit number you have entered.
  • You must verify whether the information displayed correctly or incorrectly corresponds to the premise address of the business that to be licensed by selecting the button.

If the information is correct, a message will appear on your screen and you will be able to select the button to continue to the next part of the application.

If the information is incorrect or key information is missing from the displayed data:

  • You may indicate that some or all of the information is incorrect by selecting the incorrect radio button
  • If key information (e.g., street number, street name, quadrant, zip code) is missing, the verification will automatically label the information as incorrect
  • The missing field(s) will be highlighted in bold red
  • You will be required to select the checkbox
  • A data entry screen will unfold for you to enter the full and accurate business premises address information.
  • The Home Occupancy Permit will automatically be added to your list of required Supporting Documents, where you can upload a copy.

Once you have entered all of the requested information a message will appear on your screen and you will be able to select the button to continue to the next part of the application.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
