Business Information

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is used to validate the Certificate of Occupancy (CofO) for the business to be licensed

  • Enter your Certificate of Occupancy number in the box as it appears on the document, to include letters and numbers (Older CofOs may be all numbers).
  • You may need to acquire a copy of the Certificate of Occupancy from the business owner or from the property owner if your business is a tenant in the location where you will do business.
  • Select the button.
  • A screen will unfold that displays the Business Premises address that corresponds to that Certificate of Occupancy number you have entered.
  • You must verify whether the information displayed correctly or incorrectly corresponds to the premise address of the business that is to be licensed by selecting the button.

If the information is correct, a message will appear on your screen and you will be able to select the button to continue to the next part of the application.

If you do not have a Certificate of Occupancy:

  • You will be able to select the check box
  • The system will display an error message instructing you to acquire a CofO from the Office of Zoning. You may not proceed beyond this point until you acquire a CofO.

Since you do not have a Certificate of Occupancy, you must acquire one from the Office of Zoning prior to submitting your Business License application. Please select "Return to Checklist" and visit the DC Office of Zoning at 1100 4th St., SW or call 202-442-4400.

If the CofO information is incorrect or key information is missing from the displayed data:

  • You may indicate that some or all of the information is incorrect by selecting the incorrect radio button
  • If key information (e.g., street number, street name, quadrant, zip code) is missing, the verification will automatically select the incorrect radio button
  • The missing field(s) will be highlighted in bold red
  • You will be required to select the checkbox
  • A data entry screen will unfold for you to enter the full and accurate business premises address information.
  • The Certificate of Occupancy will be added to your list of required Supporting Documents automatically, where you can upload a copy.
  • Your Application Checklist will also be updated to indicate you provided the business premises Address.

Once you have entered all of the requested information a message will appear on your screen and you will be able to select the button to continue to the next part of the application.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
