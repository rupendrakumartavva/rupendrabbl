Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is used to manually enter the Business Headquarters address for a company this is not required to register with the Corporations Division.

Enter all required information into the appropriate fields and select .

  • If your business is a Sole Proprietorship, please enter your full name (or the full name of the Business Owner) in the Business Name field AND in the First, Middle and Last Name Fields.
  • If your business is a General Partnership or a Joint Venture, please enter your business name in the Business Name field and complete the First, Middle and Last Name fields with your name (or the name of the Business Owner).

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
