Password Reset

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is used to reset your password if it is lost or needs to be changed.

Enter your registered username. If you cannot remember your username, use the Forgot Username link on the LOGIN page to recover it.

Choose how you would like to reset your password.

  • If you would like an email sent to your registered email address, select the Email option.

Click the Submit button.

Look for a "NoReply" email from dc.gov in your registered account.

Please allow five (5) minutes for delivery.

Use the link provided in the email to reset your password.

Be sure to check your Spam or Junk folder if you do not see the email in your inbox.

  • If you would like to answer your security questions, select the Security Questions option.

Click the Submit button.

One of the three (3) security questions that you previously selected will be displayed.

Type in the answer and click the Submitt Button.

If you answer any security questions incorrectly, double check your answer and try again.

If you need additional help, review the My DC Business Center User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
