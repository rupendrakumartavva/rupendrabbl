Payment

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page is to make payment to submit your application for a Basic Business License.

Select the type of credit card you will use from the pull down menu of accepted cards.

Enter your credit card number, expiration date and security code in the appropriate fields.

Enter and re-enter the email address to receive your payment receipt.

Use the radio buttons to select the billing address for your credit card. It can be the displayed address that is on file with your business license, or another address that you will be asked to type in the appropriate fields.

In order to move forward from this page you must select the box stating that you making any false statements in your application may make you subject to criminal prosecution.

Select the button to complete the transaction.

If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
