Add An Existing Business License to My DC Business Center Account

Help With This Page (Click Here)

This page provides the data entry screen for the BBL renewal information you receive in the Renewal Application Instructions and Renewal Bill sent from the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs (DCRA).


Within ninety (90) days of the expiration of your existing Basic Business License you will receive in the mail a Renewal Application Instructions and Renewal Bill from the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs.

Use this document to enter the information into the appropriate fields:

  • PIN stands for Personal Identification Number and is printed on your renewal bill.
  • License Number is the BBL license number and is printed on your renewal bill.
  • Tax Number (FEIN/SSN) ) is the Federal Employer Identification Number of your business or, in some cases, your Social Security Number. Your tax number is NOT printed on your renewal bill. Enter your tax number using dashes for the appropriate format

Select the button to search for the existing BBL that corresponds to the information you entered.

*Note* For security purposes, a BBL can only be viewed/added to one person's My DC Business Center account. Once a BBL is added to one individual's account, the license cannot be added to a second account. To change the access privileges to a BBL, contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.


If you need additional help, review the My BBLs User Guide or contact the DCRA Help Desk at (202) 442-4400.
